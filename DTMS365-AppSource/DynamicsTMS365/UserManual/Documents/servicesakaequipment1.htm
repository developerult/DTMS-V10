Services (aka equipment)

After creating your contract, you will need to configure the Service associated with this contract.Â  Service is also synonymous with equipment. For example: 1) Van 2) Reefer 3) Standard LTL 4) Truckload 5) Intermodal 6) Hopper 30 7) Priority LTL 8) Flatbed

Each contract is associated with the respective service/equipment. For example, Carrier Lee Trucking can have an LTL contract and a TL contract. Or Lee Trucking can have a Reefer Contract and a Flatbed contract.

When configuring the capacities, usually only the maximums are required. For simplicity, you can just populate the Weight Max to be 42,000 lbs. or the max value of your loads/equipment. Service/Equipment is a default configuration setting.

If you set the weight max to 20,000 lbs., this means that this tariff will not be available for any order over 20,000 lbs. the system assumes you do not have equipment to move that load. All conditions must match the capacity settings. Or if you set your truckload capacity with a minimum of 20,000 and maximum of 42,500 lbs. This tariff will not appear on smaller shipments under 20,000 lbs.
