Resource Notifications

If Override Alert is on for the Resource, notifications will be sent to the Notification Email Address.
All notifications are in reference to the Carrier Automation Self Service Portal.


No Appointments Available - Carrier attempts to book an appointment but there are no open time slots at the Resource
No Temperature Preference - Carrier attempts to book an appointment but the order(s) do not have any temperature information
No Package Type Preference - Carrier attempts to book an appointment but the order(s) do not have any package type information
Carrier Deleted Appointment - Carrier deleted an appointment scheduled at the resource via the Self Service Portal


Carrier Modified Appointment - Carrier modified an appointment scheduled at the resource via the Self-Service Portal


Carrier Booked Appointment - Carrier booked an appointment at the resource via the Self-Service Portal
