Book Appointment

1.  The carrier can book an appointment for orders by clicking “Select Appt” available in each row of the orders list.

2.  Once clicking on “select Appt” the popup window opens with the selected order details and Available Appointments list, if the appointments are available for that order as seen in the screenshot below.

3.  The carrier can book the appointment by clicking “Book Appt” button in Action column of the appropriate warehouse & the time period from the list of appointments listed.
