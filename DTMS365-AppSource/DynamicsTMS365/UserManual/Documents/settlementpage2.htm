Settlement Page

 

To begin entering in carrier freight invoices go to NEXTrack and click on the Settlement Page.

Here you will see a list of all invoices ready to be entered for each carrier that was associated with the TMS user account. A delivery date is required before any transaction will appear here. It’s best to train carriers to insert delivery date into the portal.

Users can apply filters such as Carrier Name to view carrier specific transactions.

 

 

 

Before entering invoices, it’s best to sort your work by carrier so you can insert carrier invoices carrier by carrier.

Each freight invoice should be associated with a ShipID (CNS number) from TMS and carriers should reference this number on all invoices.

Note, summary invoices are not allowed. If carriers send summary invoices for more than 1 load, user must append a -1, -2 or –A, -B at the end of their invoice entry to make this freight invoice unique.

You can search for the CNS (aka ShipID) load you want to enter in your freight invoice for. Click on the pencil icon to EDIT.

N Status = Are new records waiting for freight bill entry.

PENDING Status = waiting on TMS user to approve entries.

FAILED Status = Are freight bills that have been entered but did not pass the audit function. This requires a TMS user to review this exception.
