Active Bids

Active bids are bids that have been submitted by 1 or more carriers. Note, you will also see the same load appear in the Pending bids indicating an active bid.

As carriers place their bid, you will see each carrier’s bid by clicking and expanding the black arrow.

The display will show all carrier bids in addition to any tariffs that you may have in the system to compare bid rate against a tariff rate or even a benchmark. The tariff icon (Black icon) will indicate this is a tariff rate or the NEXTStop icon (Green icon) indicates a bid. Blue Icon indicates API rate.

The total cost column will be your all-inclusive rate from the carrier.

Click Accept to accept the Bid and award to a carrier.

An email will be sent to the carrier with the tender report and directing them to login and accept the bid. Users must have carrier contact emails configured correctly to receive tender emails. Carriers will see this tender request in the Tender page. Carriers must accept load in Tender Page.

To Remove a Bid

To remove a bid from being posted, you must click on the REMOVE button which will delete the posting from displaying on the NEXTStop Carrier screens as well as the NEXTStop Ops pending bids page.

Once clicked, the load will be set to “N” status.

Note, Bid Postings will automatically appear in the Carrier’s Portal Access under NEXTStop Carrier. No email will be sent out notifying carriers of the bid request. If required, users should send a separate email blast.
