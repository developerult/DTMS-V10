All Page

This is where carrier users can update the status of shipments and apply filters such as order number, ShipID, CNS Number, PO Number, and Assigned Carrier.

In most cases the CNS Number and the ShipID will be the same number. A ShipID will be assigned to every order. Multiple orders can be assigned to the same ShipID, for example, a multi stop load.

Users can apply filters to search for specific information. Use the drop-down list to apply filter criteria.

 

Hover your cursor over the magnifying glass to see all load status updates related to this order (load) whether the input is from EDI or carrier direct entry into the system.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Hover your cursor over the Calendar icon to review appointment information.

To see the items that are on this order, click on the black arrow to expand the page.

Click on the pencil icon to enter information such as Pick Up and Delivery appointment information, trailer number, seal number, load status comments, and more.


When shippers are using the scheduling system, these fields will auto populate. The appointment fields will be grayed out when the using the scheduling system and after an appointment is created. This is to protect the appointment for use within the scheduling system and that no one can edit the appointment when scheduling system is in use. Upon delivery, the fields will no longer be grayed out. Carriers should follow shippers’ requirements for data entry and what needs to be updated and when.
