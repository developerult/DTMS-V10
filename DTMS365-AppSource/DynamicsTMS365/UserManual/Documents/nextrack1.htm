NEXTrack

Most user configurations will have access to NEXTrack and the pages contained within. By clicking the black arrow and expanding NEXTrack, you will see other pages accessible within NEXTrack.

The help screen will appear. Each user can add their own notes and procedures directly into this screen by clicking on the pencil icon to edit the help information. Click save when done. For example, if you click on the pencil icon, Peggy and can add her notes and procedures as reminders. Peggy will only see Peggy’s notes. All users will see ALL page Help.

 

Expand the black arrow next to NEXTrack to review the pages according to your credentials.

- NEXTrack

- ALL (track and trace functions, search capability)

- Tendered (Typically for carriers and depending on carrier user credentials)

- Settlement (Typically for carriers and depending on carrier user credentials)

- Settled (Typically for carriers and depending on carrier user credentials)
