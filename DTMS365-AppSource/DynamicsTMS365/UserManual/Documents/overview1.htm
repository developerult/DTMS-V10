Overview

The major change in v-8.2.x has been the addition of the Legal Entity configuration (see the “Dynamics TMS - Legal Entity Maintenance.docx” training document for more details).  Legal Entities provide multi-tenant capabilities and support for detailed security not available before.  For existing customers upgrading to v-8.2.x one Legal Entity will be created automatically as part of the upgrade process and existing users will be assigned to this new Legal Entity.  NEXTrack Carriers Users are always assigned to the Legal Entity of  “None” and will have access to all loads tendered to them from any Legal Entity. 

In previous versions of Dynamics TMS User Maintenance and security was divided into two separate components.  The NEXTrack users and the Dynamics TMS users.  The security and maintenance of these two components has now been combined into one system. 

The new system has added a new hierarchy for security which follows this design:

Super Users: composed of NGL Support Staff and one Administrator.  Super users may be assigned to a Legal Entity, but security restrictions are not applied.

Carriers:  Legal Entity “None”

Legal Entity Warehouse: NEXTrack Only users

Legal Entity CSR Sales: NEXTrack Only users

Legal Entity Administrators: Administration of all components for a specific Legal Entity

Legal Entity Operations: General dispatching and traffic management for a specific Legal Entity

Legal Entity Accounting: Financial data and freight bill processing.

 

It is possible to create custom security roles, but it is recommended that you contact NGL support for assistance to ensure compatibility.
