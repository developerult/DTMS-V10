User Maintenance

 

Before adding a user, you need to determine what “type of user” you want to set up and the required features or functionality they will need access too. Users can be assigned to a Role according to the pre-configured Role Center setup.

CSR/Sales – users who want access to rate shopping or track & trace.
Warehouse – users who need access to scheduling and track & trace.
Operations-Typically logistics coordinators or warehouse personnel.
Accounting – Users who will be auditing and reconciling freight bills. May have same access as
                    Operations.
Administration – Full access to system
