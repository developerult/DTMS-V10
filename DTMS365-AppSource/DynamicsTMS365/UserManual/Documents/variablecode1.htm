Variable code:

The variable code is used to set up the calculation formula used to calculate the accessorial fee. The calculation formula uses the Formula Variable to determine the total cost of the fee to be applied.  The system then uses an allocation formula to determine how the total is allocated across similar locations defined by the allocation mode.

For Example: a calculation formula could be 10 percent of the carrier cost, but the allocation could be by weight across all similar destination locations. The system adds up the total carrier cost for the destination, get 10 percent, and then allocate the 10 percent by weight across all destinations.
