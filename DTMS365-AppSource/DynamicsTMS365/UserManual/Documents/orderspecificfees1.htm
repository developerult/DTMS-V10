Order Specific Fees

 

Order specific fees – are additional fees that are needed.  These are simple fees that can be added to specific orders.  They are not associated to a carrier or lane.  Select the add fees button in the ribbon bar and add the fee needed.  To delete the fee, select the fee under order specific fees and click the delete button on the keyboard. Order Specific fees are typically determined following a delivery i.e. unloading, detention, or Liftgate Service (if never originally applied).
