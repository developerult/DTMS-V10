NEXTrack

 

Most user configurations will have access to NEXTrack and the pages contained within. By clicking the black arrow and expanding NEXTrack, you will see other pages accessible within NEXTrack.

 

Expand the black arrow next to NEXTrack to review the pages according to your credentials.

- NEXTrack

- ALL Used by carriers to enter appointments, load status, delivery confirmation, and search
                 capability.

- Tendered (Used for carrier dispatchers to accept/reject loads, depending on carrier user
   credentials)

- Settlement (Used by carriers who will enter freight invoices, depending on carrier user
                 credentials)

- Settled Used by carriers to see approved invoices and payment status. Note, payment status
                 updates in the Settled page depends on shipper’s configuration and may not be used.
                 (Depending carrier user credentials)
