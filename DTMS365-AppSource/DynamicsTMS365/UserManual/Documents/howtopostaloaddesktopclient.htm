How to Post a Load -Desktop Client

1) Desktop Client – Go to the Load Detail screen. Click on Post Load.
2) The Load Board Selection screen will open. Check NEXTstop.
3) Click Post. DAT requires a separate subscription. Inquiry via support.
4) The load will go to “PC” status and Post to NEXTstop portal.
NO EMAIL NOTIFICATION IS SENT TO THE CARRIERS. USERS SHOULD SEND OUT AN EMAIL.
