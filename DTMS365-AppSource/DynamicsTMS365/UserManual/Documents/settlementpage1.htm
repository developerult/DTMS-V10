Settlement Page

The settlement page is where carriers will enter in their freight invoices and fees. Again, depending on credentials, not all carrier users or TMS users will have access to this page. Carrier users, who have been set up as accounting users, will have access to the Settlement and Settled Page for carriers to enter invoices.

The Settlement page allows carriers or TMS users to enter in freight invoices. We have two options and users should seek instructions on which option is best to use based on your implementation.

1) Quick Edit – This is used for quick entry of the Freight Invoice Number and Total Invoice Amount Only. Click Save when done.

2) Detailed Freight Bill Entry-This is used to allow carriers/users to insert additional fees as part of their invoice and apply tolerances at the fee level.

 

In order for carriers to see data and enter their freight invoice within the Settlement Page, they will be required to enter in a delivery date/time stamp in the ALL Page. Only loads with a delivery date will appear in the Settlement Page.

Carriers must enter in their invoice for each load (CNS / ShipID). Summary invoices are not allowed. In situations where carriers have a summary invoice for more than 1 load, they must append a -1, -2 or –A, -B at the end of their invoice entry to make this freight invoice unique.

 

After the Delivered-Checked In Date/Time field has been populated, Click on Settlement to enter in your freight invoices.

You can search for the CNS (aka ShipID) load you want to enter in your freight invoice for. Click on EDIT to insert your invoice.

The Detailed Freight Bill Entry Screen will open. Yellow highlighted are required fields to be entered by carriers.

 

Carriers must enter in their Invoice Number. The Line Haul and fuel will autofill. The Fuel can be edited but not the Line Haul. 

Once the invoice number has been entered, carriers can now enter in any fees associated with an invoice.

Click and expand the black arrow next to the CNS Number (ShipID) and click ADD FEE.

In most configurations, the fees will automatically display and be populated with the costing.

Select your appropriate fee that has been pre-configured and fee amount. Click Add Fee again to add additional fees.

Click SAVE icon when done entering in fees. It is important to note that once fees have been entered and saved, the invoice process is complete, and carriers cannot change anything.

After entering in your invoice, fee, and clicking save. The system will update the status of the invoice based on the TMS configuration.

There are three statuses:

Approved = the system automatically approved the entry, passed audit, and will disappear from the screen. Setting the PExp (Pay Expense workflow to AA-auto approved) in the Load Detail Screen.

Pending Approval = the system accepted carrier entry and is waiting on TMS user approval. This transaction will remain in the Pending Approval Page until a user approves them.

 

 

 

The status will be updated in TMS on all loads that are Pending Approval (PA). Setting the PExp (Pay Expense workflow to PA-Pending Approval) in the Load Detail Screen.

 

 

Failed = the system accepted the freight invoice entry as a matched transaction in TMS but has failed the audit process. Users should communicate with carriers and run Audit Report and to resolve the invoice. All transactions will need to be fixed, pass audit, and will automatically set to AA status.
