Company/Warehouse Location Contacts

To see the contacts associated with this warehouse location click on the black arrow next to the warehouse name. Company contacts are typically defined as logistics or warehousing contacts.

To edit a contact, click on the pencil icon. Remember to click the save button if any changes are made. The email field is used for email communication when web tendering loads and other notifications within the TMS system. A typical configuration would use a group distribution email address, so everyone gets the notifications such as tmsoperations@company.com
