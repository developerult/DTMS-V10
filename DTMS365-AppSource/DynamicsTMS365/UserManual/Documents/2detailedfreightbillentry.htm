2) Detailed Freight Bill Entry-

 

Where carriers or TMS users can insert invoice and any fees. Administrators must configure the fees in the Carrier Page for each carrier to make fees accessible.

To enter a freight bill, click on the pencil icon in the Actions menu and The Detailed Freight Bill Entry Screen will open.

Enter in the carrier Invoice number.

The Line Haul and Fuel will auto populate. If there are any predetermined fees associated with this shipment and if made visible, the fees will open and display here. See Carrier Page to configure fees for selection.

To add another fee, click and expand the black arrow next to the CNS Number (ShipID) and click ADD FEE.

Select your appropriate fee that has been pre-configured and fee amount. Click Add Fee again to add additional fees. If you do not see a fee that you’re trying to enter, contact Logistics or the system admin to add a fee prior to entering this invoice to have it added.

Click SAVE icon when done entering in fees. It is important to note that once fees have been entered and saved, the invoice process is complete.

Once all of the freight bills have been entered. Next, you will need to go to the Fee Approval page to ensure any fee entry that is needing approval is approved, audited, and sent to accounting for payment.
