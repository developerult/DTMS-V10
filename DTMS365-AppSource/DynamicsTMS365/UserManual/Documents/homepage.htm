Home Page

 

Administrators can insert content or edit content by going to the Home Page and clicking on Actions. It’s best to create your content in a Word Document and then copy and paste the information directly into the editor. This will preserve the content and allow for simple management of the content if it ever gets over written.

Click Edit Page Content

 

Users can update the left and right-side content with specific instructions for users, whether they’re carrier or NEXTrack users. Click Save when you’re done.
