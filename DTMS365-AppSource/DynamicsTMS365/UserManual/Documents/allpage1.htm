All Page

This is where users can check the status of shipments and apply filters such as order number, ShipID, CNS Number, PO Number, and Assigned Carrier.

 

 

 

Users can apply filters to search for specific information. Use the drop-down list to apply filter criteria.

Hover your cursor over the magnifying glass to see all load status updates related to this order(load) whether the input is from EDI or carrier direct entry into the system.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Hover your cursor over the Calendar icon to review appointment information.

 

To see the items that are on this order, click on the black arrow to expand the page.
