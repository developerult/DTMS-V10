How to Post a Load – Web Client

1) Go to Load Board in Web Client. Click Rate It in the Actions Menu.
2) Turn ON Post to NEXTstop
3) Click the Rate It save button.
NO EMAIL NOTIFICATION IS SENT TO THE CARRIERS. USERS SHOULD SEND OUT AN EMAIL.

To review Posted Loads - In the menu tree you will see NEXTStop, click and expand the black arrow.

When you enter into the page, you will see the Pending bids (bids that have been posted by TMS user), Active bids (bids that have been submitted by carriers), and Finalized bids (bids that have been awarded to carriers)
