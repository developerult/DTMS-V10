Discounts

Multiple discounts can be managed for each contract such as Weight or by State. To add Discounts to your contract, go to the Exceptions page and below Class Exceptions you will find Discounts Exceptions. Only add discounts if the discount has not been applied in the rate.

Click on Add

Enter in a name for this discount structure such as US Discount or FL (state) Discount
Enter in a rate which should be a percentage like .85 for 85%
Enter weight limit for discount if it applies.
Enter in Country (minimum requirement) or specific States etc.
Enter in Point type i.e. Direct, Any, or Interline
Enter in Effective date from / to (if applicable)

Click Save.
