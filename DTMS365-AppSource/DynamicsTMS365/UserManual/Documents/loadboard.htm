Load Board

The Load Board is where users go to assign carriers and book loads. Users can configure their screen settings by using the field chooser selection drop down box. Simply check on the box for the data field you would like to see in your grid.
 

Anytime you see the settings cog, users can configure their screen settings for that page.

Users can organize the positioning of each field name by clicking and dragging field labels to another position and releasing when you see the double arrows.

Use the Load Board Filter to easily find and sort your data.

Click on the filter button retrieve data or click on remove filter to clear the filter.
