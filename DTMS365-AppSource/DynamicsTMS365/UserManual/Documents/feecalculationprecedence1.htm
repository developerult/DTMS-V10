Fee Calculation Precedence

Fee Calculation type: fees are calculated based on one of the following settings

1.  All = combine all fees together (default)

2.  Unique = only one fee will be included based on the accessorial type.  Duplicates will be ignored and marked as overwritten For example: if there is an unloading fee at lane level and unloading at order level, only the order level unloading fee will be applied There is an order of precedence used to resolve conflict with fee calculation types.  This precedence is order, Lane, Tariff.

a.  Any fee marked as unique will only be overwritten if the exact fee exists at the order level or lane level.  If the user deletes the previous overwritten fee, it will be reset back to originals.

b.  Exception to the rule – NEXTrack self-service carrier fee submittal process may affect how these fees are calculated to be defined.

3.  Carrier over lane = carrier fees have the ability to override lane fees and will combine with order specific fees. For example: if there is a carrier unloading charge and lane unloading charge and an order unloading charge; the carrier and order unloading charge will be added together and the lane charge will be ignored, and the lane fee will be marked as overwritten. Note: the order specific fee is not required for this calculation type

4.  Lane over Carrier = lane fees have the ability to override carrier fees and will combine with order specific fees. For example: if there is a carrier unloading charge and lane unloading charge and an order unloading charge; the lane and order unloading charge will be added together and the carrier charge will be ignored, and the carrier fee will be marked as overwritten. Note: the order specific fee is not required for this calculation type

5.  Carrier over all = carrier fee will always overwrite lane and order specific fees

6.  Lane over all = lane fees will always overwrite carrier and orders specific fees
