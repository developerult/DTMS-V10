Subscription Alerts-DTMS User Profile

These are sent to any individual user who has signed up to receive the subscription alerts

Note: These are all generated by the Carrier Automation/Carrier Self Service Portal. Also, the bottom 3 are for when the Carrier actually makes the changes (booked, change, delete) NOT for when they request it.

AlertNoAppointmentAvailable - Scheduler - No appointments are available on given date

AlertOrdersNoTemp - Scheduler - Orders have no temperature preferences

AlertOrdersNoPack - Scheduler - Orders have no package type preferences

AlertCarrierDeleteAppt - Scheduler - Carrier Deleted an appointment via Self Service Portal

AlertCarrierModifyAppt - Scheduler - Carrier Modified an appointment via Self Service Portal

AlertCarrierBookedAppt - Scheduler - Carrier Booked an appointment via Self Service Portal
