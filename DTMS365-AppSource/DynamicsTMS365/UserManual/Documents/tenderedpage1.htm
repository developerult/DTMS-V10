Tendered Page

This page will display all load tenders sent to each carrier by the logistics department. Carriers will only be able to see their own load tender information. Upon tendering by the logistics group, an email will be sent to the carrier indicating a load has been tendered to them and they should login and ACCEPT/REJECT the load. It is important to inform carriers that there is an expiration time for them to accept loads. A PDF tender sheet will be attached to the email. API carriers will not receive a tender email. Only carriers will have access to the Tendered page.



Accept Load – Load will successfully save and be removed from screen. Will now be available in the ALL Page for further updates.

Reject Load- If carrier rejects load, they’re required to enter reason-click save. This helps track carrier performance and reporting.
