Tendered Page

This page will display all load tenders sent to each carrier by the logistics department. Carriers will only be able to see their own load tender information. Upon tendering by the logistics group, an email will be sent to the carrier indicating a load has been tendered to them and they should login and ACCEPT/REJECT the load. A PDF attachment should also be included with the email.

Once you’re logged in, go to the Tender page to review your tenders. Shippers may place a time constraint as to how long you have to accept/reject a load.

 

Carriers should accept/reject the load by clicking on the appropriate button.

 

If carrier Rejects a Load, they must enter a reason and click save.

All accepted loads will disappear from the TENDER PAGE and now be available under the ALL PAGE for further updates and tracking.
