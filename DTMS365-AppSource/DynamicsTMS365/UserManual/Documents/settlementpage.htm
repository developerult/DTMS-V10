Settlement Page

The Settlement page allows carriers or TMS users to enter in freight invoices. We have two options and users should seek instructions on which option is best to use based on your implementation.

1) Quick Edit – This is used for quick entry of the Freight Invoice Number and Total Invoice Amount.

Click on the Quick Edit button in the Actions menu. A dialog box will open.

 

2) Detailed Freight Bill Entry-This is used to allow carriers/users to insert additional fees as part of their invoice. Click on the pencil icon to review and add fees.

The settlement page is where carriers will enter in their freight invoices and fees. Again, depending on credentials, not all carrier users will have access to this page. Carrier users, who have been set up as accounting users, will have access to the Settlement and Settled Page for carriers.

In order for carriers to see data and enter their freight invoice within the Settlement Page, they will be required to enter in a delivery date/time stamp in the ALL Page. Only loads with a delivery date will appear in the Settlement Page.

Carriers must enter in their invoice for each load (CNS / ShipID). Summary invoices are not allowed. In situations where carriers have a summary invoice for more than 1 load, they must append a -1, -2 or –A, -B at the end of their invoice entry to make this freight invoice unique.

 

 

Click on Settlement to enter in your freight invoices. Must have entered in Delivery Date/Time in All Page first.

 

You can search for the CNS (aka ShipID) load you want to enter in your freight invoice for. Click on the pencil icon to EDIT.

N Status = Are new records waiting for freight bill entry.

PENDING Status = waiting on TMS user to approve entries.

FAILED Status = Are freight bills that have been entered but did not pass the audit function. This requires a TMS user to review this exception.

Click on the Pencil icon to enter Detailed Freight Invoice information.

The Detailed Freight Bill Entry Screen will open.

Click on the Information icon for to review the pending/failed freight bill entry. Note you will not be able to edit the freight bill after it has been entered and saved. If you made a mistake, carrier should contact the shipper.

Depending on system configuration and requirements, it is important to receive instructions from the shipper on expectations and requirements for entering freight bills. 

Carriers must enter in their Invoice Number for this shipment. Once the invoice number has been entered, carriers can now enter in fees associated with an invoice if necesssary.

Click and expand the black arrow next to the CNS Number (ShipID) and click ADD FEE. Some configurations will already display the fee as part of the original freight cost calculation. There is no need to enter this fee again. If the fee is incorrect, carriers can edit the value by placing your cursor in the cost field and enter in new value. If no more fees, click save and the system will run the audit function.

Too Add a fee:

Click Add Fee Select your appropriate fee that has been pre-configured from the drop-down list and fee amount. Click Add Fee again to add additional fees. If you do not see a fee that you’re trying to enter, contact the shipper prior to entering this invoice to have it added. At this time, you can close the page and re-enter invoice upon notice the fee has been updated. Do not click save until you have completed all entries.

When entering fees for the same stop, you only need to enter the fee one time on one of the stops.

Click SAVE icon when done entering in fees. It is important to note that once fees have been entered and saved, the invoice process is complete, and carriers cannot change anything.
