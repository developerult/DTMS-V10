Company Maintenance

 

The Company/Warehouse page manages all your warehouse locations and warehouse contact information. Click on the black arrow to see contacts for this warehouse location.

 

Use the screen filter to change your data display by clicking on the black drop-down arrow and check on/off the field(s) you want to display. This feature is on most screens.

Depending on your configuration and ERP integration, some integrations will auto create the new company location. Additional configuration is required to complete if this is your scenario.

To add a Warehouse location/Company go to the actions bar and click on Add Company.

Add all the required information, including the legal entity. Note, users should seek support before setting up a new Legal Entity.

If you click on the pencil icon next to a warehouse location it will display the edit Company/Warehouse location page. Note, if you do not see your warehouse location make sure you’re in the right Legal Entity.
