Carrier Maintenance

Carrier data is global and may be used by multiple Legal Entities.  The desktop client must be used to manage the global shared data.  Legal Entity specific settings and configuration must be maintained in the D365 portal.  A carrier must be assigned to a Legal Entity before it can be used (see the “Dynamics TMS - Carrier Maintenance.docx” training material for more details).  Each Legal Entity will assign Carriers to a dispatch type, i.e. API or NGL Tariff, as well as an account reference.  For backward compatibility existing carrier contacts are shared by each Legal Entity.  Use the Desktop Client to enter shared contact information.  Use the D365 portal page to enter Legal Entity specific carrier contacts.
