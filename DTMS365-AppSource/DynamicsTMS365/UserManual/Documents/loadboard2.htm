Load Board

The load board’s primary goal is dispatching, it works similar to rate shopping with the following enhancements.

•      Spot Rates may be entered to support negotiated rates

•      TMS User may select to select rates from Private TMS tariffs, API LTL rates or both.

•      Load information is pre-defined typically from an external source like Microsoft Dynamics, load details like packages, accessorial fees, dimensions and weight may be modified prior to requesting rates.

•      Loads may dispatched immediately or they may require carrier acceptance before dispatching.

 

All of the reports and features described above for Rate Shipping are available except that quotes are not saved and the results are only updated and stored when a load has been tendered or dispatched.
